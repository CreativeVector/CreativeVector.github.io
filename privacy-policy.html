CreativeVector Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2025

CreativeVector ("we", "us", or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

When you register or use our authentication services (via email/password or third-party providers), we collect:

  • Authentication Information: Your email address.
  • Transaction Data: Information relevant to purchases (e.g., shopping cart details).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services (including user authentication).
  • To process transactions and deliver digital products to you.
  • To communicate with you (e.g., order confirmation emails or account-related emails).

Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for our authentication and database management. Your personal information (primarily email) is stored on secure Supabase servers. We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, theft, and unauthorized use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@creativevector.store.
